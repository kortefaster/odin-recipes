Veggie Stir Fry

Description

This recipe is a quick and nutritious way to enjoy a colorful array of vegetables.
It features broccoli florets, bell peppers, carrots, onions, and garlic stir-fried in a savory soy sauce.
The vegetables are cooked until they are tender yet still retain a satisfying crunch.
This stir-fry is seasoned with salt and pepper, enhancing the natural flavors of the vegetables.
It can be served as a standalone dish or paired with rice for a more substantial meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sliced onion, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Toss in broccoli florets, sliced bell pepper, and julienned carrot. Cook for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour soy sauce over the vegetables, stir well to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, then remove from heat.
  7. Serve hot as a side dish or over cooked rice.
