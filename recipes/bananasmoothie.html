PB Banana Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is a delicious and satisfying way to start your day or refuel after a workout. It combines ripe bananas, creamy peanut butter, milk (or almond milk for a dairy-free option), Greek yogurt, and a touch of honey for sweetness.
The ingredients are blended together with ice until smooth and creamy, creating a luscious and indulgent beverage.
The combination of bananas and peanut butter provides a natural sweetness and a boost of protein, making this smoothie both tasty and nutritious. It's a perfect on-the-go breakfast or afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk for a dairy-free option)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes
